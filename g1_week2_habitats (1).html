What Is a Habitat?
A habitat is the place where an animal lives. 🏡
A habitat gives animals what they need: food 🍖, water 💧, and shelter 🏠.

What We Learned Today
🏡
Habitat
A habitat is the place where an animal lives and gets food, water, and shelter.
🌲
Forest Habitat
Foxes, bears, owls, and deer live in the forest among tall, shady trees.
🌊
Ocean Habitat
Fish, dolphins, sharks, and whales live in the big, salty ocean.
🏜️❄️
Desert & Arctic
Camels thrive in hot deserts; polar bears and penguins survive freezing arctic cold!
