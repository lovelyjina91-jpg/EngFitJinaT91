Section 1 of 10
1
Grammar Focus
Teacher Explanation — Introduce all 4 patterns on the board
⏱ 0 – 20 min
🖊
How to Teach This
Write each pattern on the board one at a time. Say the example aloud, then ask students to repeat. After each pattern, point to a student and ask them to make their own sentence on the spot before moving on.
① Adjective + Be verb
He / She is + adjective.
→ My father is tall and kind.
② Likes Expression
He / She likes + noun.
→ My mother likes cooking.
③ Adjective + Noun combo
He is a [adj] + [noun].
→ He is a funny brother.
④ Connect two sentences
He is [adj]. He likes [noun].
→ She is kind. She likes music.
⚠️ Common Mistakes to Prevent
❌ My father are tall.
✅ My father is tall.
❌ She like cooking.
✅ She likes cooking.
❌ I have a old sister.
✅ I have an old sister.
❌ He is a love father.
✅ He is a loving father.

3
Sentence Transform
Worksheet Page 1 — Use given words to build sentences
⏱ 25 – 35 min
🎯
Instructions to Students
"Use ALL the given words to write sentences. You must include every word in the box — nothing is extra. The CHALLENGE item uses more words. You have to write 3 complete sentences."
[GIVEN] tall / father / likes / basketball
→ Write 1 adjective sentence + 1 "likes" sentence.
✅ Answer
My father is tall. He likes basketball.
[GIVEN] kind / mother / likes / flowers
→ Use: "She is a _____ mother. She likes _____."
✅ Answer
She is a kind mother. She likes flowers.
[CHALLENGE] smart / funny / sister / likes / reading / drawing
→ Use ALL words. Write 3 sentences!
✅ Sample Answer
My sister is smart. She is also very funny. She likes reading and drawing.

Instructions to Students

🏁 Wrap-Up Tips
• Ask 2–3 volunteers to read their paragraph aloud.
• Collect worksheets for written feedback.
• Praise use of adj+noun combo and correct He/She likes.
• Homework: Write one more paragraph about a different family member.
